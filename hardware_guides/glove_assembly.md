# OSMO Glove Assembly Guide

Required:
- 2x base gloves
- silpoxy
- kapton tape
- recommended: acrylic cutout

1. cover back of mcu board with kapton tape

2. place glove on acrylic cutout

3. (recommended) trim seams of the gloves

4. glue sensor boards into place, be careful to route the wires correctly
 need wire routing diagram

5. tape down the wires in serpantine pattern to allow flex

6. glue silpoxy at the edges of the wires
alternate version: less robust, faster - use vhb tape

7. place outer glove on top of inner glove
